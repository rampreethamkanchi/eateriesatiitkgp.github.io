About Us

KodeinKGP, a society full of Tech Developers and UI/UX experts at IIT Kharagpur is one of its kind striving to find solutions to issues of the student community using Web Development and UI/UX skills. It conducts events to engage students in various technical domains and make them aware of the fast growing Web3.0 and Web3.0 Technologies! The Web 3.0 leverages AI, Machine Learning and blockchain technology. It is expected to achieve real-world communication. Individuals will own the data, and they will be compensated for the time they spend on the internet.
