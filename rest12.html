Tikka shops

Tikka is one of the most popular food and snacks spots of IIT Kharagpur. Due to its central location and nearness to the lecture halls in Vikramshila and main building, it enjoys a persistent crowd of people during its operation. Its open seating arena is a casual hangout location.A good place to hang out with friends while having tea and snacks. Many small eateries are there. You will find various snacks option at cheap prices from morning to night.
